Color rules

  • Primary action: green to teal gradient fill with dark text and green glow.
  • Secondary action: ghost pill with strong green border.
  • Neon accents on CTAs, eyebrows, and gradient text only. Never on body paragraphs.
  • Do not use legacy colors #0060b9 or #485668 on marketing surfaces.

03

Typography & logo

Plus Jakarta Sans + Inter.

Display carries hierarchy. Body carries clarity. Logo carries recognition.

Typography

RoleFontUse
Display / headingsPlus Jakarta SansH1 to H3, buttons, stats, eyebrows
Body / UIInterLede, FAQ, footer, captions
Spec / codeMonospaceToken names, file paths

Type scale

  • Eyebrow: 13px, uppercase, green, wide tracking
  • H1 hero: clamp 2.5 to 4.25rem, extrabold
  • H2 section: clamp 1.75 to 2.5rem, bold
  • Body: 1rem, line-height 1.6
  • Tight tracking on display. Regular on body.

Logo

Use the neon mark on dark backgrounds. Preserve SVG glow. Minimum clear space: half the mark height. Do not stretch, recolor outside tokens, or place on busy photography without a scrim.

Logo do

  • Lockup with wordmark "Collins" on dark surfaces
  • Keep neon drop-shadow treatment
  • Respect clear space around the mark

Logo don't

  • No stretch, skew, or rotation
  • No color substitutions outside token system
  • No flattening onto low-contrast imagery

05

Motion

Restrained neon motion.

Motion confirms intent. It never competes with copy or CTAs.

Principles

  • Glow pulses and crossfades reinforce the palette.
  • No infinite pulse on primary buttons.
  • Beat-synced hyperframe storytelling on demo stage.
  • Scroll reveals and stat count-up on load.

Timing tokens

TokenValueUse
--duration-fast200msNav hover, FAQ
--duration-beat420msHyperframe crossfade
--duration-slow520msStage glow transition

Animated elements

  • Demo stage: inset neon ring pulse while playing
  • Carousel dots: glow pulse on active dot only
  • Subtitle overlays: stagger fade on beat change
  • Buttons: hover lift and glow only

Reduced motion

  • Honor prefers-reduced-motion: reduce
  • Instant beat swaps, no crossfade
  • Reveals show immediately
  • Stat numbers display final values at once

06

Do's & don'ts

Agency brand guardrails.

Copy, visual, and handoff rules for every surface.

Copy: do

  • Lead with one claim and one proof point per section
  • Use "Book a strategy call" as primary CTA
  • Name the six canonical services exactly
  • Quote transparent pricing philosophy
  • Attribute testimonials to role and company

Copy: don't

  • No product signup language
  • No abstract superlatives without evidence
  • No invented software features
  • No opaque "contact sales" pricing
  • No anonymous testimonial filler

Visual: do

  • Apply neon accent tokens for CTAs and eyebrows
  • Use Plus Jakarta Sans + Inter only
  • Keep dark backgrounds and green borders
  • Preserve logo clear space and SVG glow
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences

Visual: don't

  • No legacy blue or slate palette
  • No light-mode white SaaS layouts
  • No decorative serif or display fonts
  • No neon on body paragraphs or full-width bands
  • No hardcoded hex outside token system
