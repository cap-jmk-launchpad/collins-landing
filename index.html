FAQ

Questions before we partner

How is Collins different from a big agency or in-house hire?

You get senior strategists and executors without the overhead of a global shop or the ramp time of building an internal team from scratch.

What does an engagement look like?

Most clients start with a strategy sprint, then move into a monthly retainer scoped around channels, deliverables, and clear KPIs — no open-ended hours.

Do you work with specific industries?

We specialize in B2B and DTC growth brands — typically $1M–$50M revenue with active paid and content programs ready to unify.

Can you work alongside our existing tools?

Yes. We integrate with your CRM, ad accounts, and analytics stack — or help you consolidate where fragmentation is costing you.

How quickly can we start?

Discovery calls happen within a week. Strategy sprints kick off within 2–3 weeks of signing — no multi-month procurement cycles.

What does pricing look like?

Retainers and project scopes are quoted upfront with line-item clarity. We do not nickel-and-dime for basic connectivity or senior access.
