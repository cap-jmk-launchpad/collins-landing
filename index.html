FAQ

Questions growth teams ask before switching

How is Collins different from HubSpot or Salesforce?

Collins targets agile growth teams — not enterprise customization projects. You get unified SEO, ads, email, and CRM without opaque pricing or slow support queues.

Can I migrate from my current martech stack?

Yes. Most teams connect existing ad accounts, CRM records, and email lists during onboarding. Our deployment guides walk you through each integration step.

Is pricing really transparent?

Every plan lists what you pay for upfront — usage-based tiers with no hidden connectivity fees or surprise seat charges.

Do you support SEO and paid ads in one workflow?

That’s our core differentiator. Content performance feeds ad targeting and nurture sequences automatically, so campaigns stay aligned end-to-end.

How fast can we go live?

Most teams launch their first automated workflow within 3–5 business days. Full stack migration typically completes in under two weeks.

What size team is Collins built for?

Solo marketers, small business owners, and growing agencies — anyone who needs enterprise-grade automation without enterprise overhead.
